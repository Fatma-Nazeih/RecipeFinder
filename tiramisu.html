Tiramisu

Recipe ID: 107

Description

A creamy, coffee-flavored Italian dessert made with layers of mascarpone cheese, coffee-soaked ladyfingers, and cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone Cheese – 1 cup (about 225g)
  • Ladyfinger Biscuits (Savoiardi) – 18 to 24 pieces
  • Espresso or Strong Coffee – 1 cup, cooled
  • Egg Yolks – 3 large
  • Granulated Sugar – 1/3 cup (65g)
  • Heavy Cream – 1/2 cup (120ml), whipped
  • Cocoa Powder – For dusting
  • Dark Chocolate Shavings – Optional, for garnish
  • Rum or Coffee Liqueur (e.g., Kahlua) – 1–2 tbsp (optional)

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and sugar until the mixture becomes pale and creamy.
  2. Add the mascarpone cheese to the yolk mixture and mix until smooth and well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form, then gently fold it into the mascarpone mixture.
  4. Combine the cooled espresso with the rum or coffee liqueur (if using) in a shallow dish.
  5. Quickly dip each ladyfinger biscuit into the coffee mixture — don't soak them — and layer them in the bottom of your dish or serving glasses.
  6. Spread half of the mascarpone cream mixture over the ladyfingers and smooth the top.
  7. Add another layer of dipped ladyfingers, followed by the remaining mascarpone cream.
  8. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (or overnight) to allow it to set and the flavors to blend.
  9. Before serving, dust the top with cocoa powder and garnish with chocolate shavings if desired.
