Sushi Rolls

Recipe ID: 103

Description

Fresh and flavorful sushi rolls made with vinegared rice, seafood (like salmon or tuna), avocado, and wrapped in seaweed. Often served with soy sauce and wasabi.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice – 2 cups (cooked and seasoned with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt)
  • Nori Sheets – 4–6 (seaweed sheets)
  • Fresh Salmon or Tuna – 200g (sashimi-grade, sliced into strips)
  • Cucumber – 1, julienned
  • Avocado – 1, sliced thinly
  • Rice Vinegar – 3 tbsp (for seasoning rice)
  • Sugar – 1 tbsp (for seasoning rice)
  • Salt – 1 tsp (for seasoning rice)
  • Soy Sauce – For dipping
  • Pickled Ginger – Optional, for serving
  • Wasabi – Optional, for serving
  • Bamboo Mat – For rolling (makisu)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Cook the rice according to package instructions.
  2. While the rice is hot, mix in rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Let it cool to room temperature.
  3. Place a bamboo mat on a flat surface and cover it with plastic wrap. Lay a sheet of nori (shiny side down) on top.
  4. With wet fingers, spread a thin, even layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving about 1 inch at the top edge.
  5. Arrange your fillings (sliced fish, cucumber, avocado, etc.) horizontally across the middle of the rice.
  6. Using the mat, tightly roll the sushi away from you, applying gentle pressure to shape the roll. Seal the edge with a bit of water.
  7. Use a sharp knife to slice the roll into bite-sized pieces. Wet the knife with water between cuts for clean slices.
  8. Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi. Enjoy your fresh homemade sushi!
