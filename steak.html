Grilled Steak with Garlic Butter

Recipe ID: 105

Description

A perfectly grilled, juicy steak topped with a rich garlic butter sauce and served with roasted vegetables or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye or Sirloin Steak – 2 steaks (about 1 inch thick)
  • Salt & Black Pepper – To taste
  • Olive Oil – 1–2 tbsp (for brushing)
  • Unsalted Butter – 4 tbsp
  • Garlic – 3 cloves (minced or smashed)
  • Fresh Parsley – 1 tbsp (chopped, optional)
  • Thyme or Rosemary – A few sprigs (optional for extra flavor)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Take the steaks out of the fridge and let them rest at room temperature for about 30 minutes before cooking.
  2. Pat the steaks dry with paper towels, then season generously with salt and black pepper on both sides.
  3. Preheat your grill or grill pan over high heat. Brush the steaks with olive oil.
  4. Grill the steaks for 4–5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or cook to your desired doneness. Use a meat thermometer for accuracy (130–135°F / 54–57°C for medium-rare).
  5. While the steaks are grilling, melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and optional herbs, then cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  6. Once the steaks are done, remove them from the grill and let them rest for 5–10 minutes.
  7. Drizzle the garlic butter over the steaks and sprinkle with chopped parsley (optional).
  8. Serve hot with your favorite sides like roasted veggies or mashed potatoes.
