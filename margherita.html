Margherita Pizza

Recipe ID: 104

Description

A simple yet flavorful Italian pizza topped with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough – 1 ball (enough for a 12-inch pizza)
  • Tomato Sauce – 1/2 cup (preferably made from San Marzano tomatoes)
  • Fresh Mozzarella Cheese – 125g (about 1 ball, sliced)
  • Fresh Basil Leaves – A handful
  • Olive Oil – 1–2 tbsp (extra virgin)
  • Salt – To taste
  • Flour or Cornmeal – For dusting the baking surface

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible (around 475–500°F / 245–260°C). If using a pizza stone, place it inside while preheating.
  2. On a floured surface, stretch or roll out the pizza dough into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Transfer the dough onto a pizza peel or baking sheet dusted with flour or cornmeal.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Top with slices of fresh mozzarella cheese, spaced evenly.
  6. Bake the pizza in the hot oven for about 7–10 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  7. Remove from the oven, top with fresh basil leaves, and drizzle with olive oil.
  8. Slice and serve hot. Enjoy your Margherita Pizza!
