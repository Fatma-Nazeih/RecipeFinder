Lasagna alla Bolognese

Recipe ID: 111

Description

A layered Italian masterpiece with sheets of pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles - 12 sheets (no-boil variety)
  • Ground Beef - 1 lb
  • Italian Sausage - 1/2 lb
  • Onion - 1, diced
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Tomato Paste - 2 tbsp
  • Crushed Tomatoes - 28 oz can
  • Ricotta Cheese - 15 oz
  • Mozzarella Cheese - 3 cups, shredded
  • Parmesan Cheese - 1 cup, grated
  • Egg - 1
  • Fresh Basil - 1/4 cup, chopped

Steps to Prepare

  1. Brown beef and sausage with onion and garlic. Drain fat.
  2. Add tomato paste and crushed tomatoes. Simmer 30 minutes.
  3. Mix ricotta, egg, 1/2 cup Parmesan, and basil in bowl.
  4. Spread thin layer of meat sauce in 9x13 baking dish.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella.
  6. Repeat layers, ending with noodles, sauce, and cheeses.
  7. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes.
  8. Uncover and bake 25 more minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let rest 15 minutes before serving.
