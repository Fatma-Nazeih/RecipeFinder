Classic Cheeseburger

Recipe ID: 102

Description

A juicy grilled beef patty topped with melted cheese, fresh lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, and a toasted bun. Served with crispy fries.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef Patties – 2 (about 1/4 lb or 113g each)
  • Salt & Black Pepper – To season the beef
  • Cheddar Cheese Slices – 2 slices
  • Burger Buns – 2 (toasted if you like)
  • Lettuce Leaves – A few fresh leaves
  • Tomato – 1 large, sliced
  • Pickles – Sliced (optional)
  • Onion – Sliced raw or caramelized
  • Ketchup – To taste
  • Mustard – Optional, to taste
  • Mayonnaise – Optional, to taste
  • Butter – For toasting buns (optional)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Season the ground beef patties with salt and black pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 3–4 minutes on each side, or until desired doneness.
  3. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty and cover the pan (or close the grill lid) to melt the cheese.
  4. While the patties are cooking, toast the burger buns lightly with a bit of butter on a skillet or grill.
  5. Spread ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise on the buns as desired.
  6. Assemble the burger: place lettuce on the bottom bun, followed by tomato slices, the cheesy beef patty, pickles, and onion slices.
  7. Top with the other half of the bun. Serve warm with fries or your favorite side!
