Goat Cheese Bruschetta

Recipe ID: 113

Description

A crunchy toasted baguette topped with creamy goat cheese, fresh tomatoes, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

Ingredients

  • Baguette - 1, sliced diagonally
  • Goat Cheese - 8 oz, softened
  • Cherry Tomatoes - 1 cup, diced
  • Fresh Basil - 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Garlic - 1 clove, minced
  • Olive Oil - 3 tbsp
  • Balsamic Glaze - for drizzling
  • Salt and Pepper - to taste
  • Honey - optional drizzle

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brush baguette slices with olive oil and toast for 8-10 minutes until golden.
  3. Rub toasted bread lightly with garlic clove.
  4. In a bowl, mix tomatoes, basil, 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread goat cheese on each toast.
  6. Top with tomato mixture.
  7. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and optional honey.
  8. Serve immediately.
