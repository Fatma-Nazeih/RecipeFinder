Honey Chicken Wings

Recipe ID: 112

Description

Crispy, juicy chicken wings glazed with a sweet and savory honey sauce for the perfect balance of flavors.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings - 2 lbs, split at joints
  • Honey - 1/2 cup
  • Soy Sauce - 1/4 cup
  • Garlic - 4 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1 tbsp, grated
  • Rice Vinegar - 2 tbsp
  • Sesame Oil - 1 tsp
  • Cornstarch - 1 tbsp
  • Sesame Seeds - for garnish
  • Green Onions - sliced, for garnish

Steps to Prepare

  1. Pat wings dry and season with salt. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 40 minutes, flipping halfway.
  2. Meanwhile, combine honey, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, vinegar, and sesame oil in saucepan.
  3. Bring to simmer and cook 5 minutes. Mix cornstarch with 1 tbsp water and stir into sauce.
  4. Cook sauce until thickened, about 2 minutes.
  5. Toss baked wings with sauce until fully coated.
  6. Return to oven for 5 minutes to caramelize.
  7. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions before serving.
