Pepperoni Pizza

Recipe ID: 110

Description

A classic favorite with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, gooey cheese, and spicy pepperoni slices.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough - 1 lb (store-bought or homemade)
  • Pizza Sauce - 1/2 cup
  • Mozzarella Cheese - 2 cups, shredded
  • Pepperoni - 30-40 slices
  • Olive Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Garlic Powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Dried Oregano - 1/2 tsp
  • Red Pepper Flakes - optional
  • Fresh Basil - for garnish

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) with pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. Roll out dough on floured surface to 12-inch circle.
  3. Brush dough with olive oil and sprinkle with garlic powder.
  4. Spread sauce evenly over dough, leaving 1/2-inch border.
  5. Sprinkle with cheese and arrange pepperoni slices.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Sprinkle with oregano and red pepper flakes before serving.
