Chocolate Lava Cake

Recipe ID: 106

Description

A decadent dessert with a warm, gooey chocolate center that oozes out when you cut into it. Perfect with a scoop of vanilla ice cream!

Ingredients

  • Semi-Sweet or Dark Chocolate – 100g (about 3.5 oz)
  • Unsalted Butter – 100g (about 7 tbsp)
  • Granulated Sugar – 1/2 cup (100g)
  • All-Purpose Flour – 1/4 cup (30g)
  • Eggs – 2 whole eggs
  • Egg Yolks – 2 extra yolks
  • Vanilla Extract – 1 tsp
  • Butter or Cooking Spray – For greasing ramekins
  • Powdered Sugar or Ice Cream – For serving (optional)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease 4 ramekins with butter or non-stick spray, then lightly dust with cocoa powder or flour.
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler method), or microwave in 20-second intervals, stirring each time until smooth. Let it cool slightly.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the whole eggs, egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla extract until thick and pale.
  4. Slowly pour the slightly cooled chocolate mixture into the egg mixture, whisking continuously.
  5. Gently fold in the flour until just combined. Do not overmix.
  6. Divide the batter evenly into the prepared ramekins.
  7. Bake for 12–14 minutes, until the edges are set but the center is still soft. Do not overbake — the center should be gooey!
  8. Let the cakes sit for 1 minute, then carefully invert them onto plates.
  9. Dust with powdered sugar or serve with vanilla ice cream if desired. Enjoy warm!
