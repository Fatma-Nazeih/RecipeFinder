Pancakes

Recipe ID: 114

Description

Fluffy, golden pancakes served warm with butter, syrup, and optional toppings like fruits or chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour - 1 1/2 cups
  • Sugar - 3 tbsp
  • Baking Powder - 3 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Milk - 1 1/4 cups
  • Egg - 1
  • Butter - 3 tbsp, melted
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 tsp
  • Maple Syrup - for serving
  • Fresh Berries - optional topping

Steps to Prepare

  1. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in large bowl.
  2. In another bowl, beat egg with milk, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined (lumps are okay).
  4. Heat griddle or pan over medium heat and lightly grease.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on surface and edges look set, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Flip and cook other side until golden, about 1-2 minutes.
  8. Serve warm with butter, syrup, and desired toppings.
