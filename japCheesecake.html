Japanese Cheesecake

Recipe ID: 115

Description

A soft, airy, and jiggly cheesecake with a delicate sweetness and a melt-in-your-mouth texture.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese - 8 oz, softened
  • Milk - 1/4 cup
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Egg Yolks - 3
  • Lemon Juice - 1 tbsp
  • Cake Flour - 1/4 cup
  • Cornstarch - 2 tbsp
  • Egg Whites - 3
  • Sugar - 1/3 cup
  • Powdered Sugar - for dusting

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat oven to 320°F (160°C). Line 6-inch springform pan with parchment.
  2. Melt cream cheese, milk, and butter over double boiler until smooth. Cool slightly.
  3. Whisk in egg yolks and lemon juice.
  4. Sift in flour and cornstarch, mix until smooth.
  5. In separate bowl, beat egg whites until foamy. Gradually add sugar and beat to stiff peaks.
  6. Fold meringue into cheese mixture in 3 additions.
  7. Pour into prepared pan and bake in water bath for 50-60 minutes.
  8. Turn off oven and leave cake inside with door ajar for 30 minutes.
  9. Chill for 4 hours before serving. Dust with powdered sugar.
