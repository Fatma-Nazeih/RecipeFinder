Chicken Alfredo

Recipe ID: 101

Description

A creamy, delicious Chicken Alfredo pasta dish that's easy to make and perfect for a cozy dinner.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast - 2 pieces
  • Fettuccine Pasta - 200g
  • Heavy Cream - 1 cup
  • Parmesan Cheese - ½ cup
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper - To taste

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté minced garlic.
  3. Add sliced chicken breast and cook until golden brown.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and mix well.
  5. Add Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Stir until sauce thickens.
  6. Mix in the cooked pasta and toss to coat.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your Chicken Alfredo!
