Tomato Chicken Pasta

Recipe ID: 108

Description

A delicious and hearty pasta dish featuring tender chicken, rich tomato sauce, and flavorful herbs.

Ingredients

  • Pasta - 8 oz (penne or fusilli recommended)
  • Chicken Breast - 2, diced
  • Olive Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Tomato Sauce - 1 can (15 oz)
  • Cherry Tomatoes - 1 cup, halved
  • Italian Seasoning - 1 tsp
  • Red Pepper Flakes - 1/4 tsp (optional)
  • Fresh Basil - 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Parmesan Cheese - for serving
  • Salt and Pepper - to taste

Steps to Prepare

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add diced chicken and cook until no longer pink, about 5-6 minutes. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes until they begin to soften.
  5. Pour in tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, and red pepper flakes. Stir to combine.
  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat with the sauce.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
