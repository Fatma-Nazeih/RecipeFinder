Chicken Fajitas

Recipe ID: 109

Description

A flavorful Tex-Mex dish featuring tender, spiced chicken strips sautéed with bell peppers and onions.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast - 1 lb, sliced into strips
  • Bell Peppers - 2, sliced (mixed colors)
  • Onion - 1 large, sliced
  • Olive Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Lime Juice - 2 tbsp
  • Fajita Seasoning - 2 tbsp
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Tortillas - 8 small flour tortillas
  • Sour Cream - for serving
  • Guacamole - for serving
  • Fresh Cilantro - for garnish

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken strips with fajita seasoning, lime juice, and minced garlic. Marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned, about 5-6 minutes. Remove from pan.
  3. Add remaining oil to the skillet and sauté peppers and onions until tender-crisp, about 4-5 minutes.
  4. Return chicken to the skillet and toss with vegetables for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Warm tortillas according to package instructions.
  6. Serve fajita mixture with warm tortillas, sour cream, guacamole, and fresh cilantro.
