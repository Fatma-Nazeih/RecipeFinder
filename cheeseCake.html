Cheesecake

Recipe ID: 108

Description

A rich and creamy dessert with a buttery graham cracker crust, often topped with fruit, chocolate, or caramel.

Ingredients

  • Graham Cracker Crumbs – 1 and 1/2 cups (about 150g)
  • Unsalted Butter – 1/3 cup (75g), melted
  • Granulated Sugar – 2 tablespoons
  • Cream Cheese – 4 packages (8 oz each / 900g total), softened
  • Granulated Sugar – 1 cup (200g)
  • Vanilla Extract – 1 teaspoon
  • Eggs – 4 large
  • Sour Cream – 1 cup (240g)
  • All-purpose Flour – 2 tablespoons (optional, for firmness)
  • Fresh Berries, Chocolate Sauce, or Fruit Compote – for garnish

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Lightly grease a 9-inch (23cm) springform pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the pan to form the crust. Bake for 10 minutes, then let cool.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy. Gradually add sugar and beat until fully incorporated.
  4. Add the vanilla extract and mix. Then add eggs one at a time, beating on low speed and scraping down the sides after each addition.
  5. Mix in the sour cream (and flour if using) until just combined — do not overmix.
  6. Pour the filling over the cooled crust and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until the center is slightly jiggly but the edges are set.
  8. Turn off the oven, crack the oven door, and let the cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour to prevent cracking.
  9. Remove and let cool completely at room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  10. Top with berries, chocolate sauce, or fruit compote before serving. Enjoy!
