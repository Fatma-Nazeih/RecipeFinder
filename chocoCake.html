Chocolate Cake

Recipe ID: 116

Description

A moist and decadent chocolate treat with layers of rich chocolate frosting for an indulgent experience.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour - 2 cups
  • Sugar - 2 cups
  • Cocoa Powder - 3/4 cup
  • Baking Powder - 2 tsp
  • Baking Soda - 1 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Eggs - 2
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Vegetable Oil - 1/2 cup
  • Vanilla Extract - 2 tsp
  • Boiling Water - 1 cup
  • Butter - 1 cup, softened (for frosting)
  • Powdered Sugar - 4 cups (for frosting)
  • Milk - 1/4 cup (for frosting)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch pans.
  2. Whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks.
  7. For frosting: Beat butter until creamy. Gradually add powdered sugar and milk.
  8. Frost cooled cake layers and decorate as desired.
